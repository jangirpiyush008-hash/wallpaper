Michelle Morin’s Illustrations Give us Summer Vibes

By Amanda C - July 7, 2019

Michelle Morin's illustrations convey summer vibes through warm color palettes, imagery associated with warm seasons, and an overall aesthetic that communicates relaxation, warmth, and the leisure and freedom associated with summer. Her illustrations frequently feature summer scenarios including beach scenes, warm weather activities, and outdoor enjoyment. The color palettes employed are warm and saturated, with selections of colors associated with summer including warm yellows, oranges, and blues. The visual language is warm and inviting, creating emotional resonance with viewers and evoking feelings associated with summer and warm weather.

The compositions are dynamic and engaging, with visual elements arranged to create visual interest and suggest summer activities and experiences. The work communicates positive associations with summer and warm seasons, contributing to visual culture celebrating seasonal beauty and outdoor experience. The accessibility of the visual language makes the work broadly appealing while maintaining artistic sophistication. Through illustrations conveying summer vibes, Michelle Morin has created work that celebrates the beauty and positive associations of warm seasons.
