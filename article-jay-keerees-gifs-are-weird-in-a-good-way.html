Jay Keeree’s GIFS Are Weird (In a Good Way)

By Chris L - August 6, 2019

Jay Keeree creates animated GIFs and short animations characterized by a weird, unconventional, and often unsettling aesthetic that challenges conventional approaches to animation and digital art. The animations frequently feature strange imagery, unexpected transformations, and visual scenarios that violate logical expectations, creating a surreal and sometimes disturbing viewing experience. The animation style employed is often deliberately crude or deliberately polished in unexpected ways, with stylistic choices that emphasize the weirdness of the content rather than working to make it more palatable or conventional. The color choices are often bold and sometimes garish, contributing to the overall unsettling and weird quality of the work.

The brevity of the GIF format allows Keeree to present moments of visual strangeness and transformation without the need for narrative coherence or conventional story structure. The work engages with internet culture and digital aesthetics, creating animations that feel distinctly of the digital age while exploring the possibilities of animation as a medium for expressing the weird and unconventional. The work has achieved particular popularity on social media platforms, with audiences responding to the invitation to embrace strangeness and unconventionality. Through weird GIFs and animations, Jay Keeree has created work that celebrates the strange and unconventional possibilities of digital animation.
