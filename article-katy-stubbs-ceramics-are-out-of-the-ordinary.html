Katy Stubbs’ Ceramics Are Out of the Ordinary

By Dan J - June 19, 2019

Katy Stubbs creates ceramics that are deliberately out of the ordinary, refusing conventional approaches to form and decoration in favor of innovative and distinctive approaches that push the boundaries of contemporary ceramic practice. Her ceramic works are characterized by unconventional forms, experimental glazing and surface treatment, and a willingness to violate conventional expectations about what ceramic objects should look like or how they should function. The forms she creates often challenge conventional categorization, existing somewhere between functional ware and sculptural objects. The surface treatments and glazing approaches are often experimental and distinctive, with color and texture choices that prioritize artistic expression over conventional aesthetics.

The work demonstrates sophisticated understanding of ceramic materials and techniques employed in innovative and unconventional ways. Her ceramics are exhibited in gallery contexts and collected by those who appreciate innovative and experimental approaches to contemporary ceramic practice. The out-of-the-ordinary quality of her work appeals particularly to those who value artistic innovation and individual artistic vision. Through out-of-the-ordinary ceramics, Katy Stubbs has created work that expands the possibilities of contemporary ceramic art.
