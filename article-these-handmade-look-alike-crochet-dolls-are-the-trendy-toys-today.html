These Handmade Look-Alike Crochet Dolls Are The Trendy Toys Today

By Dan J - May 7, 2019

Nathalie’s Dolls features handmade crochet dolls that are adored by children and young at heart from all over the world.

Nathalie not only creates look-alike dolls that are perfect gifts for everyone but she also makes dolls for children with special needs, something that she’s proud of.

“I specialize in creating look-a-like dolls and make sure to capture every one’s specific spirit and character, taking extra care when I create dolls for children with special needs because I know this will be the only time they will find a doll that looks just like them,” she explained.

Each piece takes at least 12 hours to complete, using many miles of yarn and thousand stitches. She carefully choose all the fabrics and materials to ensure an heirloom-worthy product that can be reserved for years to come.

“Every one of my creations is made with so much love and joy, care and warmth and attention to the smallest details. I feel so grateful for the wonderful opportunity I have to reach out and share my precious creations with the world.”
