This Photo Booth Captures The Beauty Of Birds Up-Close

By Dan J - April 26, 2019

When Lisa M. Ca, aka Ostdrossel, moved from Germany to Michigan with her husband, she observed that the birds are different than in her homeland.

So she put up a bird photo booth which consists of a camera box, a macro lens, and an attachable feeder to capture the beauty of birds up close. She also used a tiny camera equipped with a motion sensor so that when a bird comes close, the camera captures it secretly.

She has experimented with all kinds of food to lure the birds in and since setting up the booth two years ago, she already accumulated thousands of beautiful photographs of various birds. Check out her latest images.
