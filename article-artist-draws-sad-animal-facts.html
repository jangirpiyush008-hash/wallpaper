Artist Draws Sad Animal Facts

By Chris L - March 29, 2019

Sad Animal Facts is an Instagram account owned by Brooke Barker, which features hand-drawn illustrations of the animal kingdom’s unfortunate facts.

Her adorable drawings are a result of hours of research about animals which she browsed on National Geographic and books in the library. She has been illustrating animals for years now. She combines the sad truths with hilarious thoughts and reactions that will definitely make you cry with laughter. Scroll down to see her sad yet funny creations.
