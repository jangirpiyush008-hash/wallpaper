Believe In Astrology? Why Not Incorporate it Into Your Body

By Chris L - August 17, 2019

For those who believe in astrology, the idea of incorporating astrological symbols, signs, and concepts into body art—through tattoos, temporary designs, or other bodily modifications—offers a way to make personal beliefs and astrological identity visible and permanent. The practice of incorporating astrology into body art reflects broader cultural interest in astrology and the desire to express personal identity and spiritual beliefs through the body. Astrology-themed tattoos range from simple representations of zodiac symbols to elaborate designs incorporating astrological elements including constellations, planetary symbols, birth chart information, and astrological mythology. The visual language of astrology offers rich symbolic material for tattoo designs, with each zodiac sign offering distinctive visual and conceptual associations that can be incorporated into personalized designs.

The practice of tattooing astrological elements reflects the belief in astrology's significance and the desire to carry this belief permanently on the body, suggesting a commitment to astrological identity and its perceived influence on personal character and destiny. The astrological tattoo community shares designs and experiences, creating a visible network of people who identify with and value astrology as a meaningful framework for understanding self and world. Professional tattoo artists have developed considerable expertise in creating astrologically-themed designs that are visually sophisticated and personally meaningful to the wearer. The combination of astrology and body art demonstrates the power of personal belief systems to motivate creative expression and the continuing appeal of tattooing as a means of expressing identity and spiritual commitment.

Through incorporating astrology into body art, individuals create permanent visual expressions of their astrological beliefs and identity.
