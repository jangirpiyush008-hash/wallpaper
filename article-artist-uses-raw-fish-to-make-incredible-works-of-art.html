Artist Uses Raw Fish to Make Incredible Works of Art

By Kate A - May 16, 2019

Sashimi is a Japanese dish of bite-sized pieces of raw fish eaten with soy sauce and wasabi paste. This is very popular especially in Japan and this delicacy is also famous as a form of art.

Culinary artist who goes by the name of Mikyoui00, never had a job related to art but his amazing sashimi arrangement skills led him to create diverse characters and creatures including animals, birds, and cartoons.

He has a son who he wanted to teach food preparation so he decided to think out of the box and turn the learning process into a fun activity by making food art.

The artist uses various kinds of fish and other ingredients in his delicate artwork. The fun part of teaching his son is the fact that he ended up getting into it himself. Scroll down to see his wonderful creations.
