This Typography Artist Experiments with Colors, Textures, and Shapes

By Amanda C - April 9, 2020

Environmental art employs natural materials and forces to create artworks that interact with landscapes and ecological systems. Land art, earth works, and environmental sculpture engage directly with natural environments, often creating temporary works that emphasize process, impermanence, and the interaction between human creation and natural forces. Contemporary environmental art frequently engages with ecological themes, climate change, and environmental restoration. Artists working with environmental media demonstrate commitment to sustainability and respect for natural systems while creating work that generates environmental consciousness and aesthetic engagement with natural landscapes.
