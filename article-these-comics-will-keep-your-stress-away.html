These Comics Will Keep Your Stress Away

By Amanda C - May 23, 2019

4amShower is a wholesome comics created by Nashville-based artist Guy Kopsombut that will surely bring a smile on your face.

Kopsombut describes it as the “most wholesome webcomics you’ll ever find.” It’s updated every day and his illustrations are always filled with love, motivation, and encouragement. And the best part – the characters are the cutest critters.

The artist aims to empathize and make his readers feel like they are not alone in their struggles. He wants to help others unwind from the stresses of everyday life.

Through his adorable animal illustrations, Kopsombut shares the love and hope among fans around the world. 4amShower comics are like hugs in comic form, so if you’re feeling down and stressed, feel free to follow his Instagram account.
