Artist Combines Painting and Photography To Create Incredible Artworks

By Pie - June 17, 2019

An artist has developed an innovative practice combining painting and photography to create extraordinary artworks that synthesize these distinct media into unified visual statements of striking originality and conceptual sophistication. The practice typically involves creating paintings that are then photographed, or creating photographs that are then painted over or combined with paint, creating hybrid works that exploit the distinctive characteristics of both media. The resulting artworks operate simultaneously as photographs and paintings, with qualities distinctive to each medium contributing to the overall visual and conceptual impact. The technical skill required to execute these works across both media is considerable, requiring mastery of both photographic and painting techniques.

The conceptual approach of combining media raises questions about categorization, authenticity, and the relationship between different forms of visual representation. The works often address themes relevant to contemporary visual culture including the nature of representation, digital manipulation, and the constructed character of visual images. The scale and presentation of these hybrid works frequently emphasizes the monumental quality of the artistic practice. The innovation evident in combining these media in new ways contributes to contemporary conversations about artistic practice and the possibilities for expanding artistic boundaries.

Through incredible artworks combining painting and photography, this artist has created work demonstrating the continued possibilities for innovation and experimentation in contemporary artistic practice.
