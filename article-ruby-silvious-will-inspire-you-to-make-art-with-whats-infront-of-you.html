Ruby Silvious Will Inspire You to Make Art with What’s Infront...

By Dan J - April 11, 2020

Three-dimensional digital art employs specialized software to create virtual sculptures, environments, and spatial constructs existing entirely within computational space. Contemporary 3D artists employ tools including Maya, Blender, and Cinema 4D to model complex forms, create photorealistic renderings, and design immersive environments. 3D art permits artists to explore spatial relationships and sculptural possibilities unconstrained by material limitations or physical laws. The work can be exhibited as digital files, interactive installations, or rendered to physical media including 3D printing. 3D digital art represents distinctively contemporary artistic practice leveraging computational capabilities to expand artistic possibilities beyond limitations of physical media.
