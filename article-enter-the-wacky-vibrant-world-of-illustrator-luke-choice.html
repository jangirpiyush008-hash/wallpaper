Enter the Wacky, Vibrant World of Illustrator Luke Choice

By Amanda C - July 25, 2019

Luke Choice is an illustrator whose work invites viewers to enter a wacky, vibrant, and imaginatively excessive visual world characterized by surreal imagery, bold color, energetic composition, and a general sense of creative abandon and visual celebration. His illustrations are visually overwhelming in their density and complexity, with multiple visual elements competing for attention and creating an overall impression of visual abundance and imaginative excess. The color palettes are often extremely bold and saturated, with combinations that might typically be considered clashing instead creating a sense of visual excitement and energy. The character designs are distinctive and often strange, with unconventional forms and unexpected visual characteristics that contribute to the wacky and surreal quality of his work.

The compositions are dynamic and chaotic, with visual elements arranged without regard for conventional compositional principles, creating instead a sense of controlled chaos and energetic visual play. The subject matter of his illustrations frequently ventures into the absurd and surreal, with scenarios and imagery that challenge logical expectations. His work celebrates visual excess and imaginative freedom, suggesting that visual communication need not be restrained or conventional. The appeal of his work lies partly in its sheer visual energy and partly in the invitation to viewers to embrace visual chaos and imaginative excess.

Through wacky, vibrant illustrations that invite entry into an alternative visual world, Luke Choice has created work that celebrates creative freedom and visual excitement.
