Artist Combines Two Cartoon Characters To Create One Amazing Drawing

By Amanda C - March 12, 2019

Linda Bouderbala is a huge fan of pop culture characters. Besides working as an art director at Marcel WW, she loves to experiment with these fictional people like classifying them by color.

This time, the Paris-based illustrator combines her favorite cartoons into a single drawing, creating a one-of-a-kind character.

Some combinations can be recognized immediately while others are difficult. In her “Fusion Challenge,” Bouderbala asked her followers to name the two characters that are fused together. Can you identify one?
