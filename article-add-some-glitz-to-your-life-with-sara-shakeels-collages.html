Add Some Glitz to Your Life with Sara Shakeel’s Collages

By Rose K - August 10, 2019

Sara Shakeel is an artist known for creating vibrant, visually dynamic collages that add glitz, glamour, and visual excitement to the spaces and contexts in which they appear. Her collage practice involves the creative combination of diverse visual elements—photographs, printed materials, textural elements—arranged into compositions that are visually striking and emotionally engaging. The color palettes employed are often bold and saturated, with combinations that create visual impact and excitement. The compositions are dynamic and engaging, with visual elements arranged to create visual movement and draw viewer attention through the picture plane.

Shakeel's work frequently incorporates luxury and glamour aesthetics, celebrating abundance, beauty, and the visual pleasures associated with contemporary consumer culture. The collages often feature human figures, frequently female subjects, rendered with attention to beauty and aesthetic appeal. The surface quality of the collages is often dimensional and textural, with layered materials creating visual depth and tactile interest. The work celebrates the pleasures of visual abundance and decorative excess, positioning these qualities as worthy of serious artistic attention rather than dismissal or critique.

Her collages have achieved significant social media popularity, with the visually striking aesthetic translating effectively to digital reproduction and sharing. The work contributes to contemporary conversations about luxury, beauty, identity, and the possibilities of visual excess and decoration in artistic practice. Through glitzy collages that add visual excitement, Sara Shakeel has created work that celebrates beauty, glamour, and the pleasure of visual abundance.
