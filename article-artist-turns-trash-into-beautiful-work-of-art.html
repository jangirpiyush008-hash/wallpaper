Artist Turns Trash Into Beautiful Work of Art

By Amanda C - May 10, 2019

Interdisciplinary artist Martha Haversham uses street trash and natural materials in her art, and transforms them into incredible miniatures.

From dried leaves and bird feathers to candy wrappers and plastic cups, she can turn any objects into something beautiful and unique. She works with “fashion atelier of haute couture street trash and natural objects,” according to her Instagram page.

Her artistic approach and visualization of discarded materials is so remarkable that she gained global attention with her artworks.
