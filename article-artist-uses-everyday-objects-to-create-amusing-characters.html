Artist Uses Everyday Objects To Create Amusing Characters

By Pie - February 25, 2019

French artist Gilbert Legrand has a wild imagination when it comes to ordinary and everyday objects. He transformed them into interesting and amusing characters.

Currently, Legrand works on two activities: “The practice of computer graphics for the work of orders (creation of characters, hyper, covers of books, digital montages) and the realization of volume illustrations having as starting point everyday objects diverted in poetic and humorous characters,” he wrote on his website.

From autumn leaves turning to a dog, to lemons becoming a man on a bike, you can find a variety of household items from Legrand’s creations. He certainly knows how to capture the fun from his surroundings.
