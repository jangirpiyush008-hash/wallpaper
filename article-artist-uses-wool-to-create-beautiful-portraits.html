Artist Uses Wool To Create Beautiful Portraits

By Chris L - April 27, 2019

Arts can encompass a wide variety of activity. Visual art is a form of art that uses any medium to denote the artist’s idea, sentiment, and imagination. Paintings, drawings, sculptures, and photography are all under visual art.

Cozy Things Studio has a unique way of showing their creativity. They specialize in creating incredible images by weaving the wool onto art boards. They shared videos on their Instagram account showing how the wools are weave delicately until it forms into a beautiful work of art.

From the video, the artist expertly sews the image of a woman. You can see the artist carving out different parts of the portraits, just by using wool and needle.
