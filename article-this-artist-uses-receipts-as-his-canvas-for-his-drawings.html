This Artist Uses Receipts As His Canvas For His Drawings

By Pie - April 28, 2019

For Italian artist Michael Moccia, every artist should find their own style. Anything can be a canvas and he is no stranger when it comes to painting on unusual surfaces. Moccia uses every canvas available, from sales receipts to dictionary pages, for his remarkable drawings.

Moccia has been practicing his unique style for years. He draws inspiration from hyperrealism paintings that gave him the drive to explore more.

“I was very fixated with hyperrealism a few years ago. Then I started experimenting more, and now I’m making this thing of my receipts, and for now I’m really enjoying it,” Moccia shared.
