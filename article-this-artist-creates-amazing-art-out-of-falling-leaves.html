This Artist Creates Amazing Art Out of Falling Leaves

By Velvet La - April 18, 2019

Kanat Nurtazin is a paper and leaf cutting artist who started a project called “100 methods of drawing,” and five years later, has created hundreds of art using different canvases and mediums.

One of his favourites is leaf-cutting, seeing its life as fleeting like human life and he wants to give a second life to it to unveil an untold story.

“I simply imagine a bright moment that I want to share with my audience. I draw it on the leaf and cut with a razor. The most important thing is to find the best suitable place to catch with my camera at the right time and right angle. And the story has been released!” he wrote on Bored Panda.
