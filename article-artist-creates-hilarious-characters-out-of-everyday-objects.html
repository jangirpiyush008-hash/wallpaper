Artist Creates Hilarious Characters Out of Everyday Objects

By Chris L - March 23, 2019

Illustrator Sean Charmatz sees the world in a different way. He loves to create adorable and hilarious animations from pictures. He uses everyday objects and gives them life by making various cute characters.

From light bulbs and pencils, to apples and oranges, he can animate everything with his artistic skills and limitless imagination. They also tell a story that is relatable to anybody.

Charmatz is no stranger when it comes to animation. He has worked in the industry for a long time. He was a former director and writer for the iconic cartoon “SpongeBob Square pants.”
