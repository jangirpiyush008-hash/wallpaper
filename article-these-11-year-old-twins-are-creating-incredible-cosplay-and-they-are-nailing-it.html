These 11-Year-Old Twins Are Creating Incredible Cosplay And They Are Nailing...

By Amanda C - April 20, 2019

Chihiro and Chieko are 11-year old twin sisters who love to cosplay. Though they are too young for this practice, their costumes are always remarkable and almost similar to the characters they are impersonating.

The girls have been cosplaying ever since they were 3 years old and their parents are very supportive in their fun pastime. They would spend hours to find materials for the perfect costumes and then organize an Instagram-worthy photo shoots.

Their cosplay adventures earned them over 4k followers and we bet their fanbase will increase in no time as their wonderful costumes are hard to ignore.
