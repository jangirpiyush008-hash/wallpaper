Artist Creates Images of Words With Its Meaning

By Pie - May 1, 2019

Ravi Koranga is a digital artist and illustrator from New Delhi, India who applies traditional techniques using Manga Studio, Procreate Sketchbook Pro, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.

His portfolio has a wide range of themes and projects including creations of images of words with its meanings.  Koranga “does fall between font and illustrative design. For while creating the words, he designs completely new fonts for each. That translates into 180 unique typefaces. Meanwhile, he adds gentle illustrative touches that turn the word into a self-representative logo,” according to the artist.

To see more of his logos and other artworks, you can check out his Instagram page.
