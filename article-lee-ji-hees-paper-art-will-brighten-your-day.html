Lee Ji-Hee’s Paper Art Will Brighten Your Day

By Jovana A - August 31, 2019

Lee Ji-Hee is a talented paper artist from South Korea who has mastered the delicate and intricate art of paper cutting and construction to create breathtaking three-dimensional artworks that seem to defy the material's natural limitations. Her meticulous approach to her craft has earned her international recognition and a devoted following of art enthusiasts who marvel at her ability to transform flat sheets of paper into complex, layered compositions. Each piece requires extraordinary patience and precision, with Ji-Hee often spending weeks on individual works, carefully cutting, folding, and assembling paper to create depth and dimension that photographers struggle to capture adequately. Her work spans a wide range of subjects, from intricate floral designs to elaborate architectural compositions and whimsical animal forms.

What distinguishes Lee's work is her ability to use negative space as effectively as positive space, creating visual depth through strategic cutting and layering. The interplay of light and shadow across her pieces creates a constantly shifting aesthetic experience depending on the viewing angle and lighting conditions. Her sculptures are photographed extensively because the camera can capture details and perspectives that the human eye might miss when viewing the piece in person. Lee's approach to paper art has revitalized interest in traditional paper-cutting techniques that have been practiced in various cultures for centuries.

She combines these traditional methods with contemporary artistic sensibility, creating works that feel both timeless and distinctly modern. Her color choices are often subtle and sophisticated, using natural paper tones, soft pastels, and carefully selected hues to enhance the visual impact of the geometric and organic forms she creates. Through her dedication to her craft, Lee Ji-Hee has demonstrated that paper is a medium with virtually unlimited potential for artistic expression, capable of conveying complex emotions and concepts with stunning visual elegance.
