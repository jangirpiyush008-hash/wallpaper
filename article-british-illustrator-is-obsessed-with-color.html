British Illustrator is Obsessed With Color

By Dan J - June 26, 2019

A British illustrator has developed a practice distinctly obsessed with the possibilities of color and its application to create visual statements and communicative artworks. The illustrator's work is characterized by fearless and experimental color usage, bold combinations that might be expected to clash but instead create visually exciting and harmonious compositions. The emphasis on color over other formal concerns creates distinctive aesthetic sensibility and immediately recognizable artistic identity. The color choices demonstrate sophisticated understanding of color theory and the emotional and perceptual effects generated through specific chromatic relationships.

The work across various contexts emphasizes color as primary vehicle for artistic expression and meaning-making. The boldness and consistency of the color obsession contributes to the distinctive appeal of the work. Through work obsessed with color, this British illustrator has created practice that celebrates color as primary artistic concern and vehicle for creative expression.
