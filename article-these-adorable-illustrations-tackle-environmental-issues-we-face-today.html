These Adorable Illustrations Tackle Environmental Issues We Face Today

By Kate A - May 22, 2019

Online marketplace WeSupply created a cute mascot, an astronaut/polar bear duo named Cosmo & Giraffe, to raise awareness about the serious environmental issues we face today. They want to educate and encourage people to fight this crisis through their informative illustrations.

Besides making these adorable drawings, they also empower customers to make a change through conscious shopping, giving a platform to combat global warming and pollution.

For every item you buy, sell, and rent, the online company pledge to clean up one pound of trash and plant one tree by using scalable and automated solutions. Their goal is to help make our planet a better place to live.
