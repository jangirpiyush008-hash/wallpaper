Susannah Montague’s Sculptures Are a Thing of Beauty

By Kate A - June 23, 2019

Susannah Montague's sculptures stand out as works of genuine beauty and artistic sophistication, demonstrating exceptional technical skill and thoughtful artistic vision in creating three-dimensional works that engage viewers in sustained contemplation and appreciation. Her sculptural practice encompasses diverse materials and approaches, all unified by consistent commitment to beauty and formal sophistication. The forms she creates are often elegant and refined, with attention to proportion, surface treatment, and compositional relationships. The materials employed are carefully selected and thoughtfully treated, with the inherent qualities of materials celebrated rather than disguised.

The scale relationships within and across her sculptural works are carefully considered, creating visual and spatial effects that engage viewers in complex perceptual experiences. The work demonstrates exceptional understanding of three-dimensional form and spatial relationships. Her sculptures are often exhibited in gallery and outdoor contexts, where they create substantial visual presences and command contemplation. The beauty of her sculptural work emerges through thoughtful conception and meticulous execution.

Through sculptures that are genuinely things of beauty, Susannah Montague has created work demonstrating the continued power and relevance of sculpture as contemporary artistic practice.
