Check Out These Incredible Typographic Mosaic Illustrations

By Pie - March 14, 2019

Nick Misani is a freelance designer and letterer who creates amazing travel-inspired mosaic designs and compiled them in his series titled, Fauxsaics.

“I started a passion project called Fauxsaics—a series of travel-inspired, typographic mosaic illustrations,” his website reads. It also features fauxsaic works by other artists.

The series has been featured on over 50 design sites and blogs including Designboom, DesignTAXI, Abduzeedo and ADC/The One Club blog, to name a few. Scroll down to see his amazing creations below.
