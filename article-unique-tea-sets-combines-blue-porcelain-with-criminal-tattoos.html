Unique Tea Sets Combines Blue Porcelain With Criminal Tattoos

By Amanda C - June 20, 2019

Unique tea sets combine blue porcelain tradition with criminal tattoo imagery and aesthetic, creating striking juxtaposition between refined porcelain tea service traditions and the bold, edgy aesthetic of criminal tattoo culture. The tea sets feature traditional blue porcelain forms and coloration combined with imagery and aesthetic associated with criminal tattoos, creating visually striking and conceptually rich objects. The formal beauty of traditional porcelain tea service is combined with the visual and conceptual edginess of criminal tattoo imagery, creating cognitive dissonance and intellectual engagement with the objects. The blue and white color scheme traditional to fine porcelain is maintained while imagery is rendered in the bold, graphic style characteristic of tattoo art.

The work raises questions about cultural hierarchies, the relationship between refined and vernacular traditions, and the possibilities for combining aesthetic traditions typically kept separate. The objects function simultaneously as beautiful porcelain objects and as statements about cultural values and aesthetic categorization. The technical skill required to render tattoo imagery on porcelain while maintaining the formal qualities of fine ceramics is considerable. Through tea sets combining porcelain tradition with criminal tattoo aesthetics, these artists have created work that challenges conventional aesthetic hierarchies and celebrates the visual power of tattoo traditions.
