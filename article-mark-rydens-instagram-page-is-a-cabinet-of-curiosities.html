Mark Ryden’s Instagram Page is a Cabinet of Curiosities

By Amanda C - August 18, 2019

Mark Ryden's Instagram presence functions as a virtual cabinet of curiosities, showcasing an eclectic collection of artworks, inspirational images, conceptual documentation, and visual experiments that create a comprehensive portrait of an artist's creative practice and aesthetic obsessions. His feed functions as a kind of extended artist's notebook, providing insights into his interests, influences, and the processes through which ideas develop into finished artworks. The eclecticism of his Instagram feed reflects the broad range of interests and influences that inform his artistic practice, from fine art to popular culture, from historical imagery to contemporary conceptual approaches. His willingness to share works-in-progress, behind-the-scenes documentation, and personal observations creates a sense of access and intimacy that conventional gallery presentation cannot achieve.

The visual consistency of his aesthetic sensibility creates a cohesive experience despite the variety of content, with each image reflecting his distinctive approach to visual culture and artistic creation. His Instagram presence has cultivated a substantial following of admirers and artists who appreciate his visual sophistication and broad cultural engagement. The platform allows Ryden to bypass conventional gatekeeping and presentation structures, presenting his work and interests directly to audiences without intermediary institutional mediation. His approach to social media use demonstrates the potential for artists to create meaningful and substantive engagement through platforms typically associated with casual documentation and self-promotion.

Through his Instagram cabinet of curiosities, Mark Ryden has created an alternative presentation structure that documents and celebrates the depth and breadth of his artistic vision and cultural engagement.
