Aitch’s Illustrations Are Full of Splendor

By Kate A - June 30, 2019

Aitch's illustrations are full of splendor and visual richness, creating works characterized by decorative abundance, luxurious color, and meticulous detail that reward sustained contemplation and close examination. The illustrations are characterized by visual abundance and decorative richness, with ornamentation and pattern employed extensively throughout compositions. The color palettes are often luxurious and distinctive, with careful selection of colors that create visual pleasure and sophisticated chromatic relationships. The detail present in the illustrations is exceptional, with intricate patterning and careful attention to formal elements throughout the work.

The compositions are complex and layered, with visual elements arranged to create visual richness and depth. The work celebrates the possibilities for abundance and decoration in contemporary illustration practice. The splendor evident in the work reflects careful artistic conception and meticulous execution. Through illustrations full of splendor, Aitch has created work that celebrates visual richness and the pleasures of detailed, decorative artistic practice.
